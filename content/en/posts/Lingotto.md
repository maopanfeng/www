> **Core Insight**: At Lingotto, James Anderson champions "Investment as an Intellectual Act." He argues that the true driver of wealth is the long-term compounding of technological breakthroughs.

### 01 The Geometry of Returns

1. **Convexity and Asymmetry**
* Most investment frameworks fail because they optimize for the "mean." Lingotto optimizes for the "tails."
* **Strategy**: Embracing radical uncertainty in exchange for unlimited upside.


2. **The End of Mean Reversion**
* In a networked economy, path dependency and increasing returns (W. Brian Arthur) create permanent winners.



### 02 Beyond Financialism

* **Complexity Science Roots**: Anderson’s framework is deeply influenced by the Santa Fe Institute. He views companies as "Dissipative Structures" that maintain order far from equilibrium.
* **The Scale of Impact**: Analyzing how companies like Tesla or ByteDance rescale human interaction and energy consumption.
